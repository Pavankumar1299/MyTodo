How to Use

Add a Task:

To add a new task, click on the "Add Task" button. Enter the task details and submit the form.

Update a Task:

To update a task, click on the task you want to update. Edit the task details and save the changes.

Delete a Task:

To delete a task, click on the delete icon next to the task. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Mark Task as Done:

To mark a task as done, click on the checkbox or the "Mark as Done" button next to the task and update button will "disable". Then you can delete it. If you don't want to delete a task and update it uncheck the checkbox then update it.

Feel free to explore and manage your tasks efficiently using my todo app!
